FAQ

Issues related to donations, and volunteering

Who can volunteer on Educate Me?

Any person over 18 years old with valid identification, or teenagers between 13 and 16 years old accompanied by a guardian with valid identification.

What's the maximum amount I can donate?

There is no maximum amount :)

What if I get debited and my donation is not visible?

Send a message to support if the problem persists past 10 minutes with no refund made to your bank account

How will my donated book be collected?

You can send it to any Educate-Me office or drop off centre via courier services or if you have a large number of books contact us and we will get them off hands

My question is not here?

Contact us directly for any clarification. Visit our contact page for details on how to contact us.
